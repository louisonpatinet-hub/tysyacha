The method

Simple daily habit.
Real results.

1
Learn the alphabet first
Most people can read Cyrillic within a few days. The Alphabet tab guides you letter by letter with audio and memory tips.
2
Open Review every day
Your daily session introduces 20 new words and brings back words you've seen before — at exactly the right moment. 10–15 minutes.
3
Rate yourself honestly
Blackout → Wrong → Hard → Easy. The algorithm adjusts your schedule based on how you rate each word. The more honest you are, the better it works.
4
By week 10, 1000 words
The spaced repetition keeps working long after you've been introduced to all the words. They keep coming back until they're locked in.

What this won't do.

Тысяча won't make you fluent. No vocabulary trainer will. Fluency requires grammar, reading, conversation, and years of exposure. What Тысяча does is give you the vocabulary foundation — the 1000 words you need before anything else makes sense. Think of it as the first serious step. Not the whole journey.

Questions

FAQ

What exactly do I get?
A single HTML file (~240KB) that you download and open in any browser. It contains everything — all 1000 words, the spaced repetition algorithm, flashcards, quiz, alphabet guide, and study plan. No internet connection required after download.
Will my progress be saved?
Yes — progress is saved in your browser's local storage. As long as you open the file in the same browser and don't clear your browser data, your progress persists. You can also export your progress as a JSON file and import it later or on another machine.
Does audio work?
Audio uses your browser's built-in text-to-speech. It works best on macOS Safari and Chrome. On some systems the Russian voice may not be available — the app shows a notice and falls back gracefully. You can mute audio at any time.
I already know some Russian. Is this useful?
Yes — the word list tab lets you filter by week and toggle words you already know with a single click. The spaced repetition will then focus on the words you actually need to learn.
What if I don't like it?
Gumroad offers refunds within 30 days of purchase, no questions asked. Just reply to your purchase email and request one.
Is this a subscription?
No. One payment, one file, yours forever. No account, no renewal, no server that can go offline. The file will work as long as browsers support HTML.
What browser should I use?
Any modern browser works — Chrome, Safari, Firefox, Edge. For the best audio experience, macOS Safari gives the highest quality Russian voice. On Windows, Chrome works well. On some systems the Russian voice may not be installed by default — the app detects this and shows a notice, but all features except audio work fully regardless.
